About the film

Subtlety is often hard to find where there is spectacle. Hailed as the Silicon Valley of China, Shenzhen presents itself best with stories of rapid development, technological marvels and commercial success. But what of the stories that ignore the familiar appeal of the city, for perhaps less spectacular topics surrounding product development?

View source: Gearing up for Shenzhen is a collection of our experiences in Shenzhen and explores the city's capacity to contribute to a responsible Internet of Things. Our goal with this film is to translate the things that we've learned to an audience of design professionals, makers and enthousiasts and to express to them a sense of urgency for better IoT practices.

We took our concept for a fair bike rental scheme to Shenzhen to see how people would react and to see if we could have a smart bike lock made according to our requirements.
